QUESTIONS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What services do we provide for weddings?

We offer a diverse range of services including DJ & MC services, sound system setup, bespoke dance floors, lighting, and screen productions. Furthermore, we coordinate with your other vendors to guarantee seamless transitions during the event.

How is the music selection tailored to the couple's preferences?

We work closely with the couple to understand their musical tastes, preferences, and any specific songs they want included or excluded from the playlist. Whether they prefer classic RnB hits, contemporary Tamil songs, or a mix of genres, the playlist reflects their unique style and sets the right mood for each part of the wedding.

How do we handle announcements and host duties during the wedding?

As experienced wedding professionals, we are comfortable and skilled in serving as the MC for the event. From introducing the family members of the bride & groom and announcing special moments to facilitating speeches and toasts, we ensure smooth transitions and keep the flow of the event running seamlessly.

Do we provide any visuals enhancements to your screen production?

Absolutely! We provide a range of options to elevate the atmosphere of your wedding production. Whether you desire bespoke monograms for your wedding logo or fully rendered backgrounds & transitions for your wedding videos, our experienced media team, who have collaborated with the likes of artists such as AR Rahman, Anirudh, DSP, and Yuvan.

How can we opt for a unique setup style for our wedding instead of the usual setups?

To achieve a distinctive style for your wedding setup, we provide a personalised consultation to discuss your ideas, preferences, and any specific themes you have in mind. Our experienced team will then create a tailored design for you and will continue to refine the setup until you are completely satisfied.

How early do we arrive to set up for the wedding?

In general, we aim to arrive at least 1–2 hours prior to the wedding ceremony and 3–4 hours before the start of the reception. This allows ample time for us to set up our equipment and perform visuals & audio checks, ensuring that everything is arranged and functioning flawlessly before the arrival of guests.

Do we handle all the necessary paperwork and liaise directly with the venue for your event?

Yes, we manage all the essential paperwork and communicate directly with the venue on your behalf. This involves submitting Health & Safety documents and coordinating logistics. Our team takes care of everything in advance to reduce stress and ensure your event runs smoothly.

How far in advance should the couple book our services for their wedding?

It's recommended to book our services as early as possible to ensure availability, especially during peak wedding season. Ideally, aim to book at least 6 months in advance.
